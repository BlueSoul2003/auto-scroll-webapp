Auto Scroll Web App

Control webpage scrolling with ease

Quick Start (Recommended)

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

Auto Scroll

To use:

  1. Go to any webpage you want to scroll
  2. Tap the "Auto Scroll" bookmark
  3. Enter a speed (1-10) when prompted
  4. Tap anywhere on the page to stop scrolling

How to Add Bookmarklet on iPhone

  1. Open Safari and go to this page
  2. Tap the share button (square with up arrow)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add Bookmark"
  4. Edit the name to "Auto Scroll"
  5. Tap "Save"
  6. To use on any webpage:
    1. Go to the webpage you want to scroll
    2. Tap the bookmarks button (book icon)
    3. Tap "Auto Scroll"
    4. Enter speed when prompted
    5. Tap anywhere to stop scrolling
